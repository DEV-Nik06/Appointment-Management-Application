About the Project

The Appointment Management System (AMS) is a web-based platform designed to facilitate easy appointment scheduling between students and staff. It allows students to book appointments with staff members, while staff can manage their availability and approve or reject appointments efficiently.

Key Features

  • 🗓️ Students can book appointments with available staff members.
  • 👩‍🏫 Staff can manage their availability and confirm or reject appointments.
  • 🔒 Separate login for students and staff members to ensure privacy.
  • 📱 Responsive design for both desktop and mobile devices.

Technologies Used

  • Backend: Django (Python)
  • Frontend: HTML, TailwindCSS, JavaScript
  • Database: SQLite (Django's default)

How It Works

Students can log in and schedule an appointment with a staff member. Staff members can log in to view and manage their available time slots. They can accept or reject appointments based on their availability.

Why Use This System?

This system simplifies appointment scheduling for both students and staff, saving time and improving productivity. It allows seamless communication between both parties and offers better time management.
